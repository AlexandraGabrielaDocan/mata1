Bronze Elegance Package

An entry-level option for clients seeking style on a budget

  • Basic Conceptual Design Board: Visual representation of color schemes and basic furnishings
  • Space Planning: Efficient layout optimization for functionality
  • Standard Material and Finish Selection: Stylish yet cost-effective options
  • Email/Phone Consultation: Regular communication to address client questions and concerns

Silver Signature Package

A mid-range package for clients desiring a personalized touch

  • Detailed Design Concept: Customized theme and mood board reflecting client preferences.
  • Premium Material and Finish Selection: Upgraded choices for a more refined aesthetic.
  • Furniture and Decor Coordination: Curated selections to enhance the overall design
  • In-Person Consultation: Face-to-face meetings to ensure a deeper understanding of client needs

Gold Bespoke Package

A top-tier offering for clients seeking the pinnacle of luxury and exclusivity

  • Exclusive Design Concept: Tailored, high-end themes reflecting the client's unique style
  • Luxury Material and Finish Selection: Premium, custom-made choices for a truly opulent feel.
  • Artisanal and Custom Furnishings: Commissioning or sourcing unique, one-of-a-kind pieces
  • VIP Concierge Service: Priority communication, on-site visits, and personal attention throughout the entire design process

These three packages provide a range of options, allowing clients to choose the level of customization and luxury that best fits their vision and budget for a truly bespoke interior design experience with Sophia Miller.
